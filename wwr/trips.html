Adventure Awaits You! Our Trips:

The Breath-Snatcher

The breath-snatcher is our most fast-paced offering, with blistering speeds, bubbling rapids, and sharp turns that will thrill even the toughest adventure -seeker Expected time: 3hrs

The Slide of Wonders

The slide of wonders is a smooth but quick journey that provides some of the most majestic views of the stunning geography that the Southwest has to offer. Expected time: 4hrs

The Sight of the Giants

Surrounded by high canyon walls, the sight of the giants will make even the bravest and most courageous among us realize just how small they are. Expected time: 2hrs

The Salamanders' Wings

The salamanders' wings is a bit of a misnomer, seeing as there are no known salamanders that live in the area, but it is otherwise teaming with animals and other life forms. Expected time: 2hrs
