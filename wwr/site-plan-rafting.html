Purpose

Our purpose is to make the outdoors affordable and accessible to all those who have the spirit of adventure. We have adventures that are comfortable for those who have never rafted before and for those who have been rafting their whole lives. Target customers: -younger people who want to start rafting for relatively little, cost-wise -they want to start rafting as a hobby -questions to be answered: how much will this cost me? how do I book trips? when are they? who will I be going with? -we're hoping to give people who are new to rafting the experience of a lifetime, and the knowledge they'll need to start rafting on their own -better to be on the laptop, should be decently mobile-friendly
